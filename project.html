Data Science 2021 Project

By Tomi Okusanya

Topic: What are the returns to further education and are they decreasing?

My project will be a look into the financial and perceived returns of further education. Inspired by “The Effects of Human Capital on Earnings: Evidence from a reform at Colombia’s top university”, I will be looking at the returns of further education, both financially, as well as socially (looking at perceptions some have on the impact of further education).
